Overview

Qeret is an immersive, Bronze Age-inspired science fiction setting where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with advanced alien technologies. It invites players to explore a world rich with lore, political intrigue, philosophical inquiry, revolution, and boundless opportunities for role-play.
